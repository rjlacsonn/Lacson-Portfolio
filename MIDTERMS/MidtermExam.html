The 16 MBTI Personality Types

Explore each personality type based on the Myers-Briggs® model.

ISTJ – The Inspector

ISTJs are logical, detail-oriented, and extremely reliable. They value tradition and structure, often excelling in roles that require responsibility and consistency.

ISFJ – The Protector

ISFJs are nurturing, detail-oriented, and deeply loyal. They are committed to helping others and are often found supporting their communities behind the scenes.

INFJ – The Advocate

INFJs are insightful, creative, and driven by a strong sense of purpose. They aim to help others fulfill their potential and are passionate about making the world better.

INTJ – The Mastermind

INTJs are analytical, logical, and visionary. They develop long-term plans and have a deep hunger for knowledge and improvement in every area of life.

ISTP – The Crafter

ISTPs are independent and adaptable. They are natural troubleshooters who excel at figuring out how things work and enjoy hands-on activities.

ISFP – The Artist

ISFPs are quiet, sensitive, and artistic. They are highly in tune with their surroundings and express themselves through art, music, or design.

INFP – The Mediator

INFPs are imaginative and guided by strong core values. They seek meaning in everything they do and are driven by a desire to help others grow and heal.

INTP – The Thinker

INTPs are logical and love abstract theories. They're driven to understand how things work and are deeply curious about the world around them.

ESTP – The Dynamo

ESTPs are outgoing, enthusiastic, and love taking risks. They're excellent in crisis situations and are masters of quick thinking and improvisation.

ESFP – The Performer

ESFPs are fun-loving entertainers. They bring energy to any space they enter and focus on living life in the moment while spreading joy.

ENFP – The Campaigner

ENFPs are energetic, warm, and full of ideas. They value authenticity and seek to connect deeply with others and the world around them.

ENTP – The Visionary

ENTPs are clever and curious. They love mental sparring and exploring new ideas, thriving in debates and pushing boundaries with creativity.

ESTJ – The Supervisor

ESTJs are born leaders. They are practical and responsible individuals who value order and tradition, and they excel at organizing people and projects.

ESFJ – The Provider

ESFJs are warm, outgoing, and loyal. They are sensitive to the needs of others and value harmony, tradition, and responsibility.

ENFJ – The Protagonist

ENFJs are compassionate and influential. They thrive on helping others grow and often become natural mentors or public figures.

ENTJ – The Commander

ENTJs are strategic leaders, always looking for ways to improve systems. They are ambitious, decisive, and excel in leadership roles.
