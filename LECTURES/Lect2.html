What You Need to Prepare

Web Design Principles

  • Plan your site before you build it

  • Consider your audience
  • Decide what your site needs to be successful
  • Study sites of similar purpose
  • Storyboard your site before constructing

What is HTML and HTML5?

HTML stands for Hypertext Markup Language. HTML5 is the latest version and adds multimedia, semantics, and graphics support.

HTML5 Syntax and DOCTYPE

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang=\"en\">
<head>
  <meta charset=\"utf-8\">
  <title>My First HTML5 Page</title>
  <link rel=\"stylesheet\" href=\"style.css\">
</head>
<body>
  <p>HTML5 is fun!</p>
</body>
</html>
